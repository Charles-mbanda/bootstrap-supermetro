About Us

We are Public Service Vehicle COmpany offering commuters services between the the counties of Nairobi and Thika respectively.
We also have our Buses plying Nairobi - Juja, Nairobi - Kikuyu and Nairobi - Makongeni Routes.

Who We Are

We are group of matatu owners operating under the umbrella of Super Metro Ltd Sacco. Our Head office is in Nairobi Tom Mboya street-Njengi House with another office in Thika.
We are specialised in public transport mainly, however we also do parcel delivery whenever need arises.
